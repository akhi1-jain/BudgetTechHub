7 Best External SSDs You Can Buy: Portable SSDs 2025

By: Akhil Jain | Published: November 11, 2025 | Category: Computer Accessories

If you are planning to buy an external storage drive, it is better to go with an SSD instead of a traditional HDD. Although SSDs are more expensive, they offer much faster transfer speeds, better durability, and a compact form factor. SSDs also provide improved shock resistance and faster read/write performance, making them a more reliable option for everyday use. The only real downside is the price—you can get a 4TB external HDD for the cost of a 500GB SSD. But the advantages of SSDs often outweigh the price difference.

While creating this list, I considered several important factors including speed, durability, build quality, storage capacity, and overall value. Features like encryption and bundled software were also looked at, but they were not the top priority.

If you want my recommendation, I suggest going with the Samsung T7 500GB. It offers excellent performance and solid value for money. If you are looking for something more affordable, the SanDisk Extreme is also a great pick.

Final Verdict

The Transcend ESD310C is the most convenient choice, eliminating the need for cables entirely.
