The mouse is your primary weapon while PC gaming. So, you should always buy the right one. For people having a low budget, I am listing the best gaming mice under Rs. 2000. If this is your budget range for a gaming mouse, have a look at this list. Choosing the best gaming mouse under Rs. 2000 can be confusing, especially with so many options available. Whether you are into FPS, MOBA, or casual gaming, a good mouse can make a big difference in your overall experience. Here are the key things you should consider before making your purchase:

Key Factors to Consider

  • Sensor Type and DPI: Look for an optical sensor with an adjustable DPI range of 200 to 8000 for flexibility.
  • Ergonomics and Comfort: Choose a mouse that suits your hand size and grip style (palm, claw, or fingertip). Lightweight designs (around 85-95g) are often preferred for fast-paced FPS games.
  • Build Quality and Buttons: Ensure it has durable switches and at least 5–6 programmable buttons for custom controls.
  • Wired vs Wireless: At this price point, wired mice are superior as they avoid input lag. Look for a flexible or braided cable.
  • Software Support: Make sure the mouse supports configuration software (like G HUB or Synapse) to adjust DPI, reassign buttons, and set up game profiles.
